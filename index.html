VKSU ARA UG (under graduate) Admission 3rd Merit List 2020 Declared

 

Name of Post:VKSU Arrah Under Graduate (UG) Admission 3rd Merit List Download 2020
Post Update Date :06/09/2020 | 11:36 AM
Short Information :Veer Kunwar Singh University (VKSU), Arrah Are Invited Online Application Form for the Course of Under Graduate (UG) Online Application Form 2020-2023. Those Candidates Are Interested to the Following Recruitment and Completed the All Eligibility Criteria Can Read the Full Notification and Apply Online

 

Veer Kunwar Singh University (VKSU), Arrah

VKSU Under Graduate (UG) Part-1 Examination Form 2020 - 2023

Course Name – Undergraduate Course (B.A/ B.com/ B.Sc)

www.dailyresult.in

 

Important Dates

  • Starting Date – 04 May 2020
  • Last Date –25/07/2020 (Date Extend)
  • 1st Merit List Date: 09-08-2020
  • Admission Date From: 10-Aug-2020 to 14-Aug-2020
  • 2nd Merit List Date: 26-08-2020
  • 3rd Merit List Date: 06-09-2020

Application Fee

  • General/OBC/SC/ST/PH – 300 RS

Veer Kunwar Singh University (VKSU), Arrah Online Examination Form 2020-2023

Course Name – Under Graduate (UG)

Eligibility:- Class 12th (Inter) Passed From Recognized Board/ University

UG (Vocational):- If you have passed 10+2 / Intermediate with Vocational / Trade (viz. Office Management, Mechanical Servicing, Yoga and Physical Education etc.) subjects and your aggregate maximum marks are more than 500.

(यदि आपने 10 + 2 / इंटरमीडिएट वोकेशनल / ट्रेड (अर्थात कार्यालय प्रबंधन, मैकेनिकल सर्विसिंग, योग और शारीरिक शिक्षा आदि) विषयों के साथ पास किया है और आपके कुल अधिकतम अंक 500 से अधिक हैं |
